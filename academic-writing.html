The teaching of academic writing in many of our institutions leaves a lot to be desired. Yet the academic success of many of our students depends on their ability to write clearly and effectively to communicate their ideas and findings to their readers. This course has been designed to complement the efforts of institutions of higher learning and cater for those who wish to further their studies but need someone to socialise them in the academic culture from a writing perspective. The course is also suitable for school leavers wishing to upskill before entering university and those academics who need to upskill to remain employable in todayâ€™s highly competitive job market.

By the end of this course, learners will be able to:

  • Write different sections
  • Write different types of paragraphs
  • Structure academic texts
  • Use discourse markers
  • Use cohesive devices
  • Improve their writing
  • Cite and reference sources
  • Evaluate their writing

The course covers the following:

  • Analysing academic texts
  • Writing an abstract
  • Writing an introductions
  • Writing methodology chapter
  • Paragraph development
  • Different types of sentences
  • grammar
  • editing
  • the writing process
  • Critical writing
  • Paraphrasing
  • Summarising
  • Evaluating Sources
  • Academic key words in assignments
NB: This is a practical course, which is designed to foster self-directed learning.


Assessment

Learners are assessed through individual assignments, class exercises, class-based group projects and oral presentations, among others.

Duration

The duration of our short courses is five days.

NB: We provide after-training support for 1 month to consolidate the skills acquired during training and ensure transfer of training to real-life situations.

Cost: N$ 2500.00 per person (including training and materials only)
